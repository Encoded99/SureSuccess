About SureSuccess Consult

SureSuccess Consult is a leading educational consulting firm dedicated to helping students achieve their dreams of university admission in Nigeria. With years of experience and expertise, we specialize in facilitating direct admission into 200 Level through IJMB and JUPEB programs.

We understand the challenges students face in gaining admission, and we're here to make the process seamless. From exam registration (WAEC & NECO) to securing your place in top Nigerian universities, we provide comprehensive support every step of the way.

Our mission is to empower students with quality education opportunities, conducive learning environments, and personalized guidance to ensure academic success.

Entry Requirements

You qualify if you have any of the following:

✓

SSCE (WAEC/NECO)

Senior Secondary Certificate Examination results from WAEC or NECO

✓

GCE (General Certificate of Education)

O'Level or A'Level certificate holders are welcome to apply

✓

NABTEB

National Business and Technical Examinations Board certificate

✓

NCE (National Certificate in Education)

NCE holders can register for direct 200 Level admission

✓

OND (Ordinary National Diploma)

OND graduates qualify for our IJMB/JUPEB programs

✓

Diploma Holders

Any recognized diploma certificate is acceptable

✓

Awaiting Results

You can register even while awaiting your examination results
